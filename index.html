Frequently Asked Questions

How does the app track data usage?

DataMonitor reads your device's built-in network counters each time you open the app and periodically in the background. It calculates usage by comparing readings over time within your billing period.

Is my data sent anywhere?

No. All usage data stays on your device. The only network connections are speed tests you manually initiate, which go to Apple and Cloudflare endpoints.

Why do the numbers differ from my carrier?

The app reads cumulative byte counters from iOS, which reset on device reboot. While the app handles reboots gracefully, minor differences from carrier-reported usage are normal. Use the app as a guide, not a replacement for carrier data.

How do I set up my billing period?

Tap the gear icon (Settings) from the dashboard. Set your billing cycle start day, end day, monthly data cap, and warning threshold percentage.

The widget isn't updating. What should I do?

Open the main app to refresh the data. Widgets update approximately every 15 minutes. If the widget still shows stale data, try removing and re-adding it.
