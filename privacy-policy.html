Privacy Policy

DataMonitor: Usage & Speed for iOS

Overview

DataMonitor is a utility app that monitors your device's cellular and WiFi data usage. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is handled.

Data Collection

DataMonitor does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

All information the app works with — usage readings, speed test results, billing period settings, and notification preferences — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's on-device storage frameworks (SwiftData and UserDefaults). None of this data ever leaves your device or is sent to any server operated by us.

Network Usage

The app reads cumulative network byte counters provided by your device's operating system. These are anonymous, device-level statistics — they contain no information about which websites you visited, which apps used data, or any content of your network traffic.

Speed Tests

When you manually initiate a speed test, the app connects to the following third-party services to measure your connection performance:

  • Apple (captive.apple.com) — for latency measurement
  • Cloudflare (speed.cloudflare.com) — for download and upload speed measurement

These connections transmit only generic test data (random bytes) to measure bandwidth. No personal information, device identifiers, or usage data is sent. These services are governed by their own privacy policies.

Widget

The home screen widget displays your current data usage. It reads the same on-device network counters and shared settings stored locally via App Groups. No data is transmitted externally.

Analytics and Tracking

DataMonitor does not include any analytics frameworks, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies. We do not track you in any way.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with any third-party services beyond the speed test endpoints listed above. There are no ads, no social media integrations, and no data brokers.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. The only way data leaves your device is if you manually choose to export a usage report (CSV or PDF) using iOS's built-in Share Sheet — and that action is entirely under your control.

Children's Privacy

DataMonitor does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy
